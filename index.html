Bechar

Discover Bechar, a captivating city in southwestern Algeria, known for its stunning Sahara landscapes and rich cultural heritage. Visit the historic oasis town of Taghit and experience thrilling desert adventures. Bechar promises an unforgettable journey into Algeria's natural beauty and history.

Taghit

Explore this historic oasis town known for its traditional mud-brick architecture and breathtaking sand dunes.

Ksour of Kenadsa

Visit these ancient fortified villages to experience the rich history and culture of the region.

Djebel Antar

Discover the stunning rock formations and enjoy panoramic views of the desert landscape.

Grand Erg Occidental

Embark on a desert adventure in one of the largest dune fields in the Sahara, perfect for camel treks and stargazing.
